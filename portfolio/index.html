Museum AI Audio Guide

GPT-SoVITS Gemini 2.5 Voice Cloning Pipeline Engineering
The Problem

Producing audio guides for 62 exhibits traditionally takes months. Content is often static and costs are prohibitive for rapid curation.

Methodology

Built a GenAI pipeline: Raw Text → Gemini (Script Refinement) → GPT-SoVITS (High-fidelity Voice Cloning of historical figures).

Impact

Expanded content from 20 to 62 exhibits in just 2 weeks (300% boost). Delivered the museum's first AI guide.
